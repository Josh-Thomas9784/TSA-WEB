Conferences & Partnerships

National and State Conferences

Orlando Florida National Conference

Every year, the Technology Student Association (TSA) holds state and national conferences where qualifying students participate in competitions and receive awards. These events provide opportunities for students to showcase their skills, network with peers, and gain recognition for their hard work. This year alone, we had 20+ students from Lamar Consolidated High School qualified to attend the national conference in Orlando, Florida. However, attending these conferences comes with significant expenses, including travel, accommodation, and registration fees. To ensure that all our qualifying students can participate, we are seeking donations to help cover these costs. Any contribution, no matter how small, will be greatly appreciated and will help us continue to provide these enriching experiences for our students.
